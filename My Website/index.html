Shisha Lounge

Prepared carefully by our best team,shisha at Bon Vivant is one of the greatest things you will experience here.It is offered in hundrets of flavors from cappuccino to watermelon and the shisha lounge atmosphere will introduce you to a ritual linked to the turkish culture and traditions.

Mediterranean Taste

Apart from the turkish tradition,Bon Vivant helps you get in touch with international traditions too. With years of experience in european finest restaurants, chef Grey has brought into menu a a little piece of the warm mediterranean land,by creating healthy,tasty recipes with,of course, a touch of colorful magic.

Wine List

Choose from our wide range of wines and drinks,each and every one perfectly adapting to every dish in the menu.From our cantina,directly to your table with just one word.

Coffeehouse Corner

There is never enough coffee to get through the day.Visit our coffeehouse for the best,italian style,coffee you will have the chance to taste. The cozy atmosphere at Bon Vivant Coffeehouse is perfect to dive yourself into a good book while smelling your favourite coffee in the world.Just like home.

Candy Bar

Try some of our best dessert recipes from all over the world.From sugar bomb to gluten-free,from chocolate chips to strawberry cupcakes,from the simple muffins to some of the finest dessert creations, a visit at our candy bar won't leave you empty handed.
